Pennsylvania State University - College of Information Science and Technology

Research Experience for Undergrad

This was my summer research experience I did over the summer of 2023. It was an amazing experience and introduced me to the topic of NLP which has become one of my favorite topics in all of computer science. During this program, I gained invaluable experience in NLP, research question formulation, literature surveying, and academic paper writing. The program helped to assure me that I wanted grad school to be my next step and that I wanted to pursue further education specifically in the topics of AI, ML, NLP, and/or cybersecurity.

My research was on the topic of automated authorship obfuscation and the viability of utilizing a psycholinguistic theory known as Uniform Information Density. I created three different algorithms that automated the obfuscation process of a large quantity of text. After obtaining the results (with some help from a graduate student at PSU), I analyzed & discussed the success of the algorithms. While my results were not anything groundbreaking due to some technical issues, the experience of conducting in-depth research on an advanced topic like NLP was immensely educational.

Favorite Reads

  • Alan Turing: The Enigma - I read this because I wanted to learn more about the Alan Turing as a person.   • Song of Ice and Fire Series - Being a massive fan of Lord of the Rings, I was eager to jump into this series. While I just recently finished the third book and I am patinetly waiting to pick-up the fourth, I can tell the series will be one of my favorite reads of all time. Typically, I struggled to get into the readings required for classes in high school but this book was different. I was entrhalled and ended up reading the whole book in a day! While the technology might not be the same as ours, the lessons one can gleam from the tragic state of their soceity can most certainly be applied to our soceity. A beautifully tragic story that I need to read again soon.
